## One of many examples of Jana's agency

In 2022, I left a prestigious academic career in the UK to return to the Czech Republic and run
a family business I'd started back in 2016. I ran it for almost a decade before selling it in
2025. Those years taught me how much weight possessions and obligations actually carry — and
that letting go of prestige and things, not accumulating more of them, was what actually got me
closer to being happy.

That decision wasn't made for me. I didn't wait for circumstances to force my hand, and I didn't
spend years quietly tolerating something that wasn't working. I named the actual problem,
decided what needed to change, and acted on it — even though the easier, more conventional path
would have been to keep what I'd built and call it success. That's also the backdrop to why I'm
now rebuilding my career around AI: starting over, deliberately, on my own terms, rather than
letting the next chapter just happen to me.
